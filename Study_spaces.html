Study Spaces

Find the best places to focus and succeed in your studies. Explore available study spaces across campus.

Top Study Locations

Hatcher Graduate Library

This library offers quiet zones, study rooms, and collaborative spaces for group work. Open 24/7 during finals week.

Location: 913 S University Ave, Ann Arbor, MI

Duderstadt Center

A perfect spot for engineering students, featuring computer labs, collaboration rooms, and tech support.

Location: 2281 Bonisteel Blvd, Ann Arbor, MI

How to Use These Spaces

Most study spaces are available on a first-come, first-served basis, but some locations, such as the Duderstadt Center, allow reservations. Visit studyspaces.umich.edu to learn more.

Study Tips

  • Use noise-canceling headphones for focus.
  • Take breaks every 25-30 minutes to stay productive.
  • Pair study sessions with group discussions to reinforce concepts.
